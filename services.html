Services

Signature Management Unit delivers specialized training and support focused on signature reduction, force protection, and operational survivability in contested digital and physical environments.

Training engagements are typically three or five days in duration and are conducted under discreet, government-funded arrangements. Instruction combines foundational concepts with hands-on, field-based application led by experienced practitioners.

SMU also supports selective equipment initiatives, including hardened devices, configurations, and operational concepts intended to reduce exposure, attribution, and risk during sensitive activities.

All services are designed to complement ISR’s doctrinal and educational mission, ensuring continuity between research, training, and real-world application.

About

Signature Management Unit was founded in 2020. The name is a direct nod to our origins in Special Mission Units within the U.S. military, reflecting both the pedigree of our practitioners and the nature of the problems we address.

We are a small team of professionals with backgrounds spanning special operations, intelligence, cyber, and applied research. Since our founding, we have provided training, advisory support, and select equipment to government clients operating in high-risk and contested environments.

Our work exists at the intersection of operational practice, emerging technology, and doctrine — translating hard-earned lessons from the field into practical capability for modern conflict.

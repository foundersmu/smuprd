Careers

Signature Management Unit works with a small cadre of experienced professionals who support training, education, and applied field instruction for government partners.

We are primarily interested in contracting with current or former special operations personnel and other practitioners with demonstrated experience in signature reduction, force protection, cyber privacy, irregular warfare, or closely related disciplines.

Engagements are typically short-duration and mission-focused, supporting discreet training events and applied education rather than permanent staffing roles.

Qualified individuals interested in supporting SMU training efforts should initiate contact via secure communications on Signal: @SMU.14

Institute for Signature Reduction (ISR)

The Institute for Signature Reduction (ISR) is an independent 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to advancing education, research, and doctrine related to signature reduction, digital force protection, and survivability in modern conflict.

ISR serves as a long-term vehicle for developing and carrying forward practitioner-informed doctrine related to irregular warfare and strategic competition, ensuring continuity beyond individual units, deployments, or personnel cycles.

SMU operates as ISR’s authorized training and implementation partner, translating ISR’s work into applied education, field training, and operational capability for government partners.

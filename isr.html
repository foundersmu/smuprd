The Institute for Signature Reduction (ISR) is a non-profit research and education organization focused on the study and advancement of signature reduction within the context of irregular warfare and strategic competition.

ISR develops doctrine, educational frameworks, and research products that examine how observable signatures — physical, digital, electromagnetic, and behavioral — shape modern conflict.

SMU serves as ISR’s authorized training and implementation partner, carrying ISR’s work forward into applied education and practitioner-led engagements with government and institutional partners.

Together, ISR and SMU form a durable ecosystem that connects academic inquiry, practitioner insight, and operational relevance.
